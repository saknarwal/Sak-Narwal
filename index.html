Meet Dr. Sakwinder Narwal

Born from a passion for both science and human connection, Dr. Sak Narwal pursued dentistry as a way to meaningfully improve people's lives — one smile at a time. He earned his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree with honors and went on to complete advanced certifications in cosmetic dentistry, implantology, and clear aligner therapy.

Based in the heart of New York City, Dr. Narwal's practice reflects the city itself — diverse, dynamic, and driven by excellence. He takes time to understand every patient's history, anxieties, and goals before recommending any treatment. Patients across Manhattan, Brooklyn, and Queens trust Dr. Sakwinder Narwala for everything from their first check-up to complete smile transformations.

Beyond the clinic, Dr. Narwal actively volunteers at New York City's free dental health camps, bringing quality oral care to communities that need it most across all five boroughs.
